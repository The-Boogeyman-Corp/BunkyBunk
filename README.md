# Privacy Policy for BunkyBunk

**Last updated:** 18-01-2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **The BunkyBunk App** ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles user information.  
By using this App, you agree to the terms outlined below.

---

## 1. Important Disclaimer

**BunkyBunk is an independent and unofficial application.**

- The App is **not owned, operated, authorized, maintained, or endorsed** by any college, university, or educational institution.
- The App does **not claim ownership, authority, or control** over any university or college login system.
- The App is created **solely for student convenience**, allowing users to:
  - View attendance details
  - Calculate attendance margins (e.g., how many classes can be missed)

The App makes use of a **publicly accessible official university student login portal**, which users already have legitimate access to.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### a. College Login Credentials

The App requires users to enter their **own college/university login credentials** to retrieve attendance information.

**Clarifications:**
- Credentials are **entered voluntarily by the user**
- Credentials are **never stored, saved, logged, cached, or shared**
- Credentials are used **only temporarily during the active session**
- We have **no access** to user credentials at any time

All authentication occurs directly between the **user’s device and the official university portal**.

---

### b. Attendance Information

- Attendance data is fetched **directly from the official university portal**
- The data is displayed **locally on the user’s device**
- Attendance data is used **only to calculate attendance-related metrics**
- No attendance data is stored on external servers

---

## 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

BunkyBunk does **not collect, store, or process**:

- Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Cookies or tracking data
- Analytics or advertising data

The App does **not operate any backend server or database**.

---

## 4. How Information Is Used

Information is used strictly to:

- Authenticate the user with the official university portal
- Display attendance details
- Perform attendance calculations for user convenience

The App does **not** use data for:
- Advertising
- Analytics
- Marketing
- Profiling
- Tracking

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- All data processing occurs **during runtime**
- No information is stored permanently
- No data is transmitted to developer-owned servers
- Once the session ends, all retrieved data is discarded

---

## 6. Data Sharing

We **do not share any data** with:

- Third parties
- Advertisers
- Analytics providers
- Educational institutions
- Government agencies

The App only communicates with the **official university portal**, initiated explicitly by the user.

---

## 7. Third-Party Services

BunkyBunk does **not integrate** any third-party SDKs or services that collect user data.

---

## 8. User Responsibility

By using BunkyBunk, users acknowledge that:

- They are using **their own authorized student credentials**
- They have legitimate access to the university portal
- The App is a **helper tool**, not an official academic service

Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with their institution’s policies.

---

## 9. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for **college and university students**.  
We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.  
Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated date.
